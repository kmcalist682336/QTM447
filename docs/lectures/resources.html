### Textbooks and Online References

#### Machine Learning Foundations
* [Elements of Statistical Learning](https://hastie.su.domains/ElemStatLearn/) by Hastie, Tibshirani, and Friedman

* [Introduction to Statistical Learning](https://www.statlearning.com/) by James, Witten, Hastie, and Tibshirani

#### Deep Learning
* [Deep Learning](https://www.deeplearningbook.org/) by Goodfellow, Bengio, and Courville
* [Neural Networks and Deep Learning](http://neuralnetworksanddeeplearning.com/) by Michael Nielsen
* [Dive into Deep Learning](https://d2l.ai/) - Interactive book with code examples

* [Bayesian Reasoning and Machine Learning](http://web4.cs.ucl.ac.uk/staff/D.Barber/pmwiki/pmwiki.php?n=Brml.HomePage) by David Barber
